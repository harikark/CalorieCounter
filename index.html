Candy Calorie Counter

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four different chocolates below. By clicking on a chocolate you will add a specific amount of calories to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to random number (which should be your max daily calorie intake), you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each chocolate is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each chocolate.
